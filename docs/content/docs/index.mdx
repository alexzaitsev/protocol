A self-hosted [MCP server](https://modelcontextprotocol.io/) that gives Claude (or any MCP-compatible AI client) a persistent, versioned record of your supplement history and health profile. AI-first.

## What it does

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="💊 Supplement lifecycle">
    Every change is a new record, never an edit. Using [SCD Type 2](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slowly_changing_dimension#Type_2:_add_new_row) versioning you can reconstruct your exact protocol on any past date.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🎯 Purpose tracking">
    Each supplement carries a purpose list so the AI always knows why you take what you take - not just what.
  </Card>
  <Card title="📋 Active protocol view">
    Query your current stack at any time, ordered by time-of-day block, with dosages and full inventory details.
  </Card>
  <Card title="🩺 Health profile">
    Conditions, medications, diet, activity, and health priorities - stored once, applied to every response.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

See the **[roadmap](roadmap/roadmap/)** for planned features.

## How it works

<Steps>
1. **Deploy the server**

   Set up Supabase for the database and Fly.io for hosting. Run the deploy workflow to push migrations and update the server.

2. **Connect your AI client**

   Add Protocol as a connector in Claude.ai or any MCP-compatible client. Authenticate with your Google account.

3. **Populate your data**

   Run the onboarding prompt to fill in your health profile, preferences, and supplement history through a guided conversation.

4. **Ask**

   Your AI now has full context - supplement history, health profile, preferences. Ask questions, record changes, review history, all through conversation.
</Steps>
