Example prompts to get you started. These work in any chat within your Protocol project.

## Daily use

- "What do I take this morning?"
- "Give me my full protocol for today."

## Managing supplements

- "I started taking magnesium glycinate 400 mg every evening for sleep. Add it."
- "I'm reducing my fish oil from 2 g to 1 g daily - update it."
- "I stopped taking vitamin C. End it."
- "Add vitamin D3 2000 IU to my inventory - it's a softgel by Nordic Naturals."

## Reviewing history

- "Why did I start taking zinc?"
- "Show me the full history of my fish oil entries."
- "What was my protocol 3 months ago?"

## Updating your profile

- "I was just diagnosed with hypothyroidism - add it to my conditions."
- "I moved to London. Update my location."
- "I want shorter, more direct responses from you. Update my communication preference."
