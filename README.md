# Protocol

A supplement protocol engine with blood test correlation and full change history - designed for a household of 1 or more people who track what they take, why they take it, and whether it's working.

The system runs as an [MCP server](https://modelcontextprotocol.io/) on Fly.io with a Supabase PostgreSQL backend. There is no GUI - you interact with your data entirely through AI assistants like Claude.
